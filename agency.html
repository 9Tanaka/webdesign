Hololive Production (ホロライブプロダクション)
Hololive Production (ホロライブプロダクション hororaibu purodakushon, stylized in lowercase), or simply known as hololive (ホロライブ), is a Virtual Talent agency consisting of Virtual YouTubers owned by Japanese tech entertainment company COVER Corporation.
Re:AcT
Re:AcT, formerly known as KAGAYAKI STARS (KAGASTA, カガスタ for short), is an agency of Virtual YouTubers created by mikai inc. As of February 2022, the agency has over 1.273 million total subscribers on YouTube. Hanabasami Kyo has the highest subscriber count out of the agency with over 300,000 subscribers.
Veepigenesis (VEE)
Veepigenesis, also shortened and marketed as VEE, is a Japanese Virtual YouTuber agency owned and operated by Sony Music Entertainment Japan (SMEJ). This is SMEJ's largest virtual talent development and management project in their history. They plan on debuting on more than 50 virtual talents, and in addition to distribution and video production, they will be involved in music, voice acting, creative work, and other activities to make their "dreams" come true.
NIJISANJI Project (にじさんじプロジェクト)
NIJISANJI Project (にじさんじプロジェクト), colloquially known as just NIJISANJI, is an agency of primarily 2D Virtual YouTubers who are also known as "Virtual Livers" (バーチャルライバー Bācharu Raibā) due to posting on sites outside of YouTube, such as NicoNico, Twitch and Bilibili.
V&U (Virtual & United)
V&U is a South Korean-based VTuber agency for virtual idols from another world. "At V&U, we're creating one-of-a-kind virtual experiences through working with virtual entertainers, connecting the virtual 2D world to our 3D world. Just as time passes in our world, our talents grow and change as well! Together, we can enjoy a nice summer vacation, or have fun during the holidays, and surpass the dimensional barrier."
NoriPro (Tsukudani Norio Production)
NoriPro is a Japanese VTuber agency founded by manga artist Tsukudani Norio, with its name short for "Tsukudani Norio Production." As of January 2024, it ranks as Japan's 8th largest VTuber agency on YouTube, boasting over 1.3 million subscribers. Unlike most agencies that hold open auditions, NoriPro initially featured only talents personally selected by Tsukudani Norio, many of whom had existing creative backgrounds. However, in September 2023, the agency reopened public auditions for the first time since 2019, expanding opportunities for new creators.
MAHA5 (Mahapanca)
MAHA5, read as Mahapanca (महा "maha" and पञ्च "panca" being Sanskrit for "great five"), is a VTuber agency based in Indonesia and is part of Rentracks Indonesia. Their goal is to connect Indonesia with Japan though anime and otaku culture while having fun together.
V-SEA
V-SEA was a Virtual Youtuber agency based in Singapore. It use to consist of two generations, Gen 0 and Gen 1, for a total of six talents, along with at least two staff members.
KemoV (Kemono Friends V Project)
KemoV, also known as the Kemono Friends V Project, is a VTuber spinoff of the Kemono Friends franchise produced by KADOKAWA. It features "Friends," cute anthropomorphized animal girls who live in Japari Park. The Kemono Friends series spans anime, manga, games, and live events, and often collaborates with zoos and wildlife organizations to promote education and conservation. KemoV's VTubers stream on YouTube using 3D avatars based on their original character designs, occasionally referencing the wider Kemono Friends universe while remaining accessible to new viewers.
VOLs (Virtual 'Ohhh!' Live Streaming!)
VOLs or Virtual 'Ohhh!' Live Streaming! is a English-language, Filipino focused, esports-oriented Vtuber agency operated by Singapore-based CRAZIA Pte. Ltd.
